MVP Blueprint: Cell & Gene Therapy Orchestration Platform

Anonymized product blueprint showing the 13-step workflow, system integrations, roles, data flows, and capability status for an orchestration platform built during the first CAR-T BLA launch. Original created during initial launch preparation.

Order & Scheduling

Partner receives order and schedules resources; Platform manages courier

Apheresis

Kit arrives at APH center, patient arrives, collection starts and ends, kit is picked up

Manufacturing

MFG receives kit, conducts cell therapy, creates, tests, ships finished product

Infusion

Delivered and re-infused

1Call center operator receives order from physician and manually enters it into ERP
2Partner services manually schedules patient, APH Center, and Manufacturer capacity
3ERP pushes data to Platform, and Platform schedules shipping via Courier API
4Platform tracks COC/COI and sends notifications to appropriate parties
5Courier readies shipper and APH kit, delivers to APH Center before patient's arrival
6APH nurse scans kit label and confirms patient ID
7Apheresis happens. Nurse performs collection on patient, enters notes.
8Courier picks up APH product from APH center
9Manufacturer receives APH kit from courier. ERP scans kit, pushes data to Platform
10Manufacturing happens, and Platform knows to schedule a pickup
11Courier picks up FP from manufacturer
12Manufacturing prints APH report, adds to official paper batch record
13Courier delivers FP to POC infusion center and scans package
